# CGL Page Guidance

Use this guidance when the line of business is commercial general liability.

Good page types:

- Commercial general liability declarations
- Limits schedule
- Classification and territory schedule
- Exposure/rating schedule
- Additional insured endorsement
- Products-completed operations endorsement
- Exclusion endorsement
- Aggregate limit endorsement
- Premium audit/accounting conditions

Synthetic example style:

> Classification and territory entries are used for rating and do not determine
> coverage by themselves. The applicable coverage part, limit, exclusion, and
> endorsement must be read with the declarations.

> A form listed for an exposure item may apply to only the scheduled operation,
> location, or completed-operations hazard shown in the declarations.

Avoid:

- Accident examples
- Litigation advice
- Real ISO form text
- Dense rating tables as the primary document body
