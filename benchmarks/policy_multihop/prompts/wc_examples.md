# Workers Compensation Page Guidance

Use this guidance when the line of business is workers compensation.

Good page types:

- Workers compensation and employers liability information page
- Classification schedule
- Premium and payroll audit conditions
- Experience rating notice
- Waiver of recovery endorsement
- Voluntary compensation endorsement
- State amendatory endorsement
- Officer/member inclusion or exclusion notice

Synthetic example style:

> The classification schedule assigns estimated remuneration to the states and
> class codes shown. Final premium may change if payroll records, job duties, or
> state assignments differ from the estimates used at issuance.

> The experience modification factor and schedule rating entry apply to the
> policy as a whole unless a state-specific endorsement provides otherwise.

Avoid:

- Medical claim narratives
- Statutory quotations
- Real NCCI/state bureau text
- Overly neat benchmark-like row descriptions
