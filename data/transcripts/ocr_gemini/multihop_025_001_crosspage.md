Loss Cause Classification Appendix

Internal reference page used by claim intake and reserving systems. The definitions below are written as filing instructions rather than as a data
export.

BKD is assigned to Liability when the intake narrative describes Insured vehicle backed into fixed object or parked vehicle. Files coded this way are
normally routed to Auto Liability Unit unless the reserve authority note requires manager review.
______________________________________________________________________________________________________

RER is assigned to Liability when the intake narrative describes Insured vehicle rear-ended another vehicle. Files coded this way are normally routed to
Auto Liability Unit unless the reserve authority note requires manager review.
______________________________________________________________________________________________________

TRN is assigned to Liability when the intake narrative describes Wide turn or lane change contact with another vehicle. Files coded this way are normally
routed to Complex Liability Desk unless the reserve authority note requires manager review.
______________________________________________________________________________________________________

ROL is assigned to Physical Damage when the intake narrative describes Rollover or loss of control damage to insured vehicle. Files coded this way are
normally routed to Physical Damage Desk unless the reserve authority note requires manager review.
______________________________________________________________________________________________________

CGO is assigned to Cargo when the intake narrative describes Cargo shortage, damage, contamination, or spoilage. Files coded this way are normally
routed to Cargo Claims Desk unless the reserve authority note requires manager review.
______________________________________________________________________________________________________

IMD is assigned to Inland Marine when the intake narrative describes Equipment or transported vehicle damage during handling. Files coded this way
are normally routed to Inland Marine Unit unless the reserve authority note requires manager review.
______________________________________________________________________________________________________

REV appears in older extracts as a review marker. It is not a claim cause and should not be used to classify current losses.
