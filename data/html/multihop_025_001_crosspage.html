Internal reference page used by claim intake and reserving systems. The definitions below are written as filing instructions rather than as a data export.

BKD is assigned to Liability when the intake narrative describes Insured vehicle backed into fixed object or parked vehicle. Files coded this way are normally routed to Auto Liability Unit unless the reserve authority note requires manager review.

TRN is assigned to Liability when the intake narrative describes Wide turn or lane change contact with another vehicle. Files coded this way are normally routed to Complex Liability Desk unless the reserve authority note requires manager review.
ROL is assigned to Physical Damage when the intake narrative describes Rollover or loss of control damage to insured vehicle. Files coded this way are normally routed to Physical Damage Desk unless the reserve authority note requires manager review.
CGO is assigned to Cargo when the intake narrative describes Cargo shortage, damage, contamination, or spoilage. Files coded this way are normally routed to Cargo Claims Desk unless the reserve authority note requires manager review.
